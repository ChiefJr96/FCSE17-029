Gaborone Maitisong Festival

In its 33rd year running, the Maitisong Festival is undoubtedly the biggest arts festival in Botswana. International and local artists will bring theatre, music, dance, poetry and comedy to audiences in and around Gaborone.

Khawa Dune Festival

The Khawa Dune Challenge & Cultural Festival event in Khawa features quad and motor bike challenges, fun Camel rides, Cultural performances and exhibitions and sale of arts and crafts from the Kgalagadi area.

Mmakgodumo Heritage & Cultural Festival

This annual festival pays tribute to Ngwaketse culture, while promoting exchanges with other local cultures in the interest of national unity. Venued in Kanye over three days in early October.

Ngwao Boswa Festival

Celebrations during this festival are rural, taking place in Nlapkhwane, (northeastern Botswana) and include traditional Tswana dancing, games and yummy homemade cooking.

Botswana Airshow

The WesBank Botswana International Airshow will be spreading it's wings in 2021 with a bigger, better and more exiting event combining the ever popular Airshow with an exhilarating Air Race.

DIACORE Gaborone Marathon

The Diacore Gaborone Marathon is a marathon in Gaborone, Botswana. The marathon is governed by the International Association of Athletics Federations and the Botswana Athletics Association.

Kalahari Botswana Desert Race

Botswana has been home to the Toyota 1000 Desert Race since 1991 traversing thousands of kilometres in several areas including Gaborone, Lentsweletau, Hatsalatladi with last stop being Jwaneng.

KURU Dance Festival

This annual celebration encourages a cultural interchange amongst the performers and the people, as the festival welcomes other cultural performers from diverse ethnic groups and countries.

Okavango Delta Music Festival

Situated in the floodplains of the Delta swamps, enjoy three days of live local and international bands, delicious food, eco-art and set up camp in the most beautiful scenery for blissful fun.

Global Expo Botswana

Botswana Investment and Trade Centre (BITC) will this year host its fifteenth edition of its premier business to business (B2B) multi -sectorial expo known as the Global Expo Botswana.
